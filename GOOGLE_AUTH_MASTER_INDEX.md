## 📍 THE ANSWER YOU NEED

### "Where do I add the Google Client ID?"

```
FILE:     frontend/.env.local
LINE:     REACT_APP_GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID=YOUR_ID_HERE
FORMAT:   123456789-abc...apps.googleusercontent.com
```

**That's it!** ✅
